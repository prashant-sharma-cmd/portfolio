My RC Plane Project

About the Project

Welcome to my simple page discussiong about RC Plane Project. This project combined my fascination with RC planes into an real life practice. In the workshop, I designed and built an RC plane with an impressive glide ratio of 1:6. This means for every meter the plane descends, it covers six meters horizontally, showcasing its remarkable efficiency in flight.

From conceptualization to execution, this project has been a journey of creativity and technical exploration. Below, you can find photos capturing key moments and a video showcasing the plane in action.
